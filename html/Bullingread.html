The Bulling

by 2S/A Presentation Team

9min 53sec
Written By Kyrlos Adel
Genre: Community issues
Released: 2025

In this book, we will explore bullying from all angles, starting with understanding its types and causes to examining its deep impact on individuals and society. We will present case studies and real-life stories to illustrate its real-world effects, along with practical tips and effective strategies to combat and prevent bullying. Additionally, we will discuss the role of parents, teachers, and decision-makers in creating a safer and more supportive environment. Our goal is to equip readers with the knowledge and tools they need to stand against bullying and promote a culture of respect and empathy in all areas of life.
